# Welcome to the ToolTime Pro Beta 👋

Thanks for helping us shape ToolTime Pro — the all-in-one app for running a field
service business (scheduling, quotes, invoices, payments, your booking site, and
Jenny, your AI assistant).

You're an early tester. Some things will be rough — that's exactly what we want
to hear about. **There are no wrong answers and you can't break anything.**

## Getting started (5 minutes)

1. Go to **[your beta URL]** and click **Sign up**.
2. Enter your name, business name, and email.
3. Walk through **onboarding** — set up your services and business hours.
4. When prompted to choose a plan, use this **test card** (no real charge):
   - Card: `4242 4242 4242 4242`
   - Expiry: any future date · CVC: any 3 digits · ZIP: any
5. You'll land in your **dashboard**. You're in. 🎉

## What we'd love you to try

Pick whatever fits how you actually run your business:

- **Booking** — share your booking link and book a test appointment
- **Quotes** — create a quote (try Jenny's "describe the job" / photo-to-quote)
- **Invoices** — turn a quote into an invoice and send it
- **Schedule** — drag jobs around the calendar, assign a crew member
- **Jenny AI** — ask Jenny to help with a quote, a question, or a follow-up
- **Your website** — preview the booking site it builds for you
- **Free tools** — the calculators and compliance checklist under **Tools**

## Known limitations during beta

- **Text messages (SMS) are turned off.** Reminders, confirmations, and 2FA come
  by **email** for now — we're finishing carrier approval for texting. Don't
  worry if a text doesn't arrive; that's expected.
- This is a beta environment — **don't enter real customer or payment details.**
  Use the test card above and made-up customer info.
- You may hit the occasional rough edge or placeholder. Tell us!
