7. EMPLOYEE RIGHTS

As an employee in California, you have the right to:

  • Be paid at least the minimum wage for all hours worked
  • Be paid overtime (1.5x) for hours over 8 in a day or 40 in a week
  • Be paid double time (2x) for hours over 12 in a day
  • Take meal periods (30 min unpaid for shifts over 5 hours)
  • Take rest breaks (10 min paid for every 4 hours worked)
  • Receive an itemized wage statement each pay period
  • Be paid on your regular payday
  • Receive final pay immediately upon discharge or within 72 hours if you quit
  • Be free from retaliation for exercising your rights

Questions or Complaints? Contact the Division of Labor Standards Enforcement (DLSE) at 1-844-522-6734 or visit www.dir.ca.gov/dlse
