New Hire Checklist

Complete this checklist for all new team members

Employee Information

Employee Name
Start Date
Position/Role
Worker Type (W-2 / 1099)

Pre-Hire Documentation

  • Signed Offer Letter W-2
    Must include at-will statement, compensation, start date, and CA required disclosures
  • Independent Contractor Agreement 1099
    Must pass ABC test requirements under California AB5
  • Background Check Authorization
    If required for position - must comply with CA ban-the-box law

First Day Forms W-2 Employees

  • Form I-9 (Employment Eligibility)
    Section 1 completed on first day; Section 2 within 3 business days
  • Form W-4 (Federal Tax Withholding)
    Employee completes and signs
  • CA Form DE-4 (State Tax Withholding)
    Required for California employees
  • Wage Theft Prevention Notice (DLSE Notice)
    REQUIRED: Must be provided at time of hire per Labor Code 2810.5
  • Direct Deposit Authorization
    Optional - employee may choose paper check
  • Emergency Contact Form
    Name, relationship, and phone number

First Day Forms 1099 Contractors

  • Form W-9 (Taxpayer ID Request)
    Required for 1099 reporting; keep on file
  • Certificate of Insurance
    Verify contractor carries own liability/workers comp if required

Equipment & Access

  • Company Vehicle Keys / Access
    If applicable - document vehicle ID number
  • Tools & Equipment Issued
    List all items on equipment checkout form
  • Uniform / Safety Gear
    Shirts, hats, safety glasses, gloves, etc.
  • Building/Office Keys or Access Code
    Document key numbers or access credentials
  • ToolTime Pro App Login
    Worker app for time tracking and job assignments

Training & Orientation

  • Company Policies Review
    Attendance, conduct, safety, and anti-harassment policies
  • CA Sexual Harassment Prevention Training
    Required within 6 months for employers with 5+ employees
  • Safety Training
    Job-specific safety procedures and equipment usage
  • Time Tracking Procedures
    How to clock in/out, breaks, overtime rules
  • Job Procedures Training
    Service standards, quality expectations, customer interaction

Systems Setup

  • Add to Payroll System
    Enter all tax forms and payment info
  • Report New Hire to CA EDD
    Required within 20 days via e-Services or DE 34 form
  • Add to Communication Channels
    Team group chat, email list, scheduling system
  • Create Personnel File
    Store all signed documents securely
