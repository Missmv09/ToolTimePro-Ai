# Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions for ToolTime Pro. If your problem isn't listed here, contact support@tooltimepro.com or call 1-888-980-8665.

---

## Account & Login

### Can't log in
- **Check your email and password** — Use the email you signed up with
- **Reset your password** — Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- **Check your email** — Look for the password reset link (check spam/junk too)
- **Clear browser cache** — Stale sessions can cause login loops
- **Try a different browser** — Rules out browser-specific issues

### Session keeps expiring
- Default session timeout is **30 minutes** of inactivity
- Compliance and HR pages have an extended **60-minute** timeout
- Click **Stay Logged In** when the timeout warning appears
- If you're frequently timing out, check your browser isn't blocking cookies

### Can't access a feature
- Some features are **plan-gated** — check [Pricing & Plans](Pricing-and-Plans) to see what's included
- Some features require **specific permissions** — ask your account owner to update your role
- **Dispatch Board** and **Route Optimizer** require the Elite plan
- **Jenny Pro** requires the Elite plan or an add-on purchase

## Jobs & Scheduling

### Jobs not showing for workers
- Verify the job is **assigned** to the worker (not just created)
- Check the job **date** — workers see today's jobs by default
- Have the worker **refresh** their app (pull to refresh)
- Confirm the worker's **account is active** in Team settings

### Double-bookings
- Enable the **smart calendar** in booking settings — it prevents overlapping time slots
- Check for manually created jobs that bypass the booking system
- Review worker availability settings

### Recurring jobs not generating
- Verify the recurring job is **active** (not paused)
- Check the **schedule pattern** (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Ensure the **end date** hasn't passed

## Invoicing & Payments

### Customer says they can't pay
- Verify the invoice was **sent** (check status in Invoices)
- Ask the customer to check **spam/junk** for the invoice email
- **Resend** the invoice from the dashboard
- Ensure your **Stripe account** is connected and active in Settings → Payments

### Payment not showing up
- Stripe payments can take **1–2 minutes** to sync
- Check your **Stripe dashboard** directly for the payment
- If using QuickBooks Sync, wait for the next sync cycle
- Contact support if payment is stuck in "processing"

### QuickBooks not syncing
- Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** and check the connection status
- **Reconnect** QuickBooks if the token expired
- Verify you're syncing to the correct QuickBooks company
- Check that the invoice/customer exists in QuickBooks

## Worker App & GPS

### GPS clock-in not working
- **Enable location services** in the phone's settings for the browser
- Ensure the phone has a **GPS signal** (works poorly indoors sometimes)
- Try stepping **outside** for a clearer signal
- Check that **location permissions** are set to "Allow" or "While Using"

### Photos won't upload
- Check **internet connection** — photos queue and upload when connected
- Ensure the **camera permission** is granted to the browser
- Try **reducing photo size** if using very high-resolution camera settings
- Clear browser cache and try again

### Worker can't see the app
- Ensure the invite email was **sent** (check Team → Members)
- Have the worker check **spam/junk** folder
- **Resend** the invite from the Team page
- Verify the worker's email address is correct

## Jenny AI

### Jenny Lite not responding on website
- Check that Jenny Lite is **configured** in dashboard → Jenny Lite
- Verify your website has the **Jenny chat widget** code installed
- Test in an **incognito window** to rule out cache issues
- Check that your plan includes Jenny Lite (Pro and Elite plans)

### Jenny Pro not answering calls
- Verify your **Jenny Pro** add-on is active (Settings → Billing)
- Check that the **phone number** is configured correctly
- Test by calling the number yourself
- Contact support if calls go to voicemail unexpectedly

## Online Booking

### Booking page not showing services
- Ensure services are marked as **Active** in Settings → Services
- Check that **Online Booking** is enabled in your settings
- Verify your booking page URL: `tooltimepro.com/book/[your-company-id]`
- Try accessing in an incognito window

### Customers not receiving confirmations
- Check the customer's **email address** for typos
- Ask them to check **spam/junk** folders
- Verify **email notifications** are enabled in Settings

---

## Quotes

### Customer can't view the quote
- Verify the quote status is **Sent** (not Draft)
- **Resend** the quote from the Quotes page
- Ask the customer to check **spam/junk**
- Test the quote link yourself by clicking "Preview"

### E-signature not working
- Ensure the customer is using a **modern browser** (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Try on a **different device** (phone vs computer)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Contact support if the issue persists

## Billing & Account

### Can't change my plan
- Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
- Plan changes take effect at the **next billing cycle**
- If you see an error, ensure your **payment method** is current
- Contact support for help with plan changes

### Charge I don't recognize
- Check **Settings** → **Billing** for your subscription details
- Review any **add-ons** you may have activated
- Annual plans charge once per year — check the billing date
- Contact support@tooltimepro.com with the charge details

## Performance & Browser

### Dashboard loading slowly
- **Clear browser cache** and cookies
- Try a **different browser** (Chrome recommended)
- Check your **internet connection**
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere
- If on mobile, ensure you have a strong signal

### Features look broken or misaligned
- **Hard refresh** the page (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
- Clear browser **cache**
- Ensure your browser is **up to date**
- Try an **incognito window** to rule out extension conflicts
