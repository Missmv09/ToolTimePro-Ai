# ToolTime Shield — Compliance & Legal Protection

ToolTime Shield is ToolTime Pro's built-in compliance suite that protects your business from costly labor law violations, worker misclassification lawsuits, and HR compliance gaps. Included in every plan.

---

## What Is ToolTime Shield?

Service contractors face serious legal risks:
- **Worker misclassification** fines can reach $25,000+ per worker
- **Wage and hour violations** lead to back-pay, penalties, and lawsuits
- **Missing HR documentation** creates liability exposure

ToolTime Shield gives you the tools to stay compliant without hiring an attorney or HR consultant.

---

## Core Features (All Plans)

### Worker Classification Quiz
- Answer a series of questions about your workers
- Get a clear recommendation: **W-2 Employee** or **1099 Contractor**
- Based on IRS guidelines and state-specific rules (AB5, economic reality test)
- Document your classification decision for legal protection

### Final Wage Calculator
- When a worker leaves, calculate their final paycheck
- Accounts for accrued PTO, unpaid wages, and state-specific rules
- California employers: meets strict final wage timing requirements

### AB5 Compliance Checklists
- Step-by-step checklists for California's ABC Test
- Ensure your contractors actually qualify as independent
- Reduce risk of misclassification lawsuits

### HR Document Library (10+ Templates)
- Worker agreements (W-2 and 1099)
- Non-disclosure agreements
- Safety policies
- Anti-harassment policies
- Equipment use agreements
- Termination checklists

## Best Practices

1. **Run the classification quiz** for every new worker before their first day
2. **Enable break tracking** for all W-2 employees, especially in California
3. **Set up certification alerts** 30 days before expiration
4. **Review compliance dashboard monthly** (Elite) to catch issues early
5. **Keep HR documents signed and on file** for every worker
