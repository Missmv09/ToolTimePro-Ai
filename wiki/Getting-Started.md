# Getting Started with ToolTime Pro

Welcome to ToolTime Pro! This guide walks you through setting up your account, completing onboarding, and dispatching your first job.

---

## 1. Create Your Account

1. Go to [tooltimepro.com](https://www.tooltimepro.com) and click **Get Started Free**
2. Enter your company name, your name, email, and password
3. Select your industry (60+ supported — landscaping, plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing, cleaning, and more)
4. Choose your plan: **Starter** ($49/mo), **Pro** ($79/mo), or **Elite** ($129/mo)
5. Verify your email and log in

> **Tip:** Annual plans save you 2 months free. A $49/month Starter plan is just $490/year.

---

## 2. Complete the Onboarding Checklist

After your first login, you'll see a **Getting Started Checklist** on your dashboard. Complete each step to unlock the full platform:

- [ ] **Add your company details** — Logo, address, phone, business hours
- [ ] **Set up your services** — Add your service catalog with pricing (see [Adding Services](Adding-Services))
- [ ] **Invite your team** — Add workers and set permissions (see [Team Management](Team-Management))
- [ ] **Connect payments** — Link Stripe to accept credit card payments
- [ ] **Set up online booking** — Enable your public booking page for customers
- [ ] **Create your first job** — Schedule and dispatch a job to a worker

---

## 3. Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your command center. Here's what you'll see:

| Section | What It Shows |
|---------|---------------|
| **Today's Jobs** | Jobs scheduled for today with status |
| **New Leads** | Recent lead inquiries from booking or Jenny AI |
| **Pending Quotes** | Quotes awaiting customer approval |
| **Unpaid Invoices** | Outstanding invoices needing payment |
| **Revenue Trend** | Revenue chart over time |
| **Today's Schedule** | Timeline view of the day's work |
| **Recent Leads** | Latest customer inquiries |

### Quick Actions
- **Dispatch Board** (Elite) — Drag-and-drop crew assignments with real-time map
- **Schedule** — Calendar view of all jobs
- **Route Optimizer** (Elite) — Automatic efficient routes for crews
- **Online Booking** — Manage your public booking page

---

## 4. Create Your First Job

1. Click **Jobs** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ New Job**
3. Select a customer (or create one)
4. Choose a service from your catalog
5. Set the date, time, and assign a worker
6. Click **Save** — the job appears on the worker's mobile app

---

## 5. Set Up Jenny AI

Jenny AI is included in every plan at no extra cost (Jenny Lite). She handles:
- **Website chatbot** — Captures leads 24/7 in English and Spanish
- **FAQ answering** — Responds to common customer questions
- **Appointment booking** — Books jobs directly from chat

Upgrade options:
- **Jenny Pro** ($49/mo) — AI phone answering, SMS messaging, direct booking, emergency escalation
- **Jenny Exec Admin** ($79/mo) — Compliance advisor, HR law monitoring, workforce analytics

Navigate to **Jenny Lite** in the sidebar to configure your chatbot.
