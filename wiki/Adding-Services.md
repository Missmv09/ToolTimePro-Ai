# Adding Services

Your service catalog is the foundation of quoting, invoicing, and online booking in ToolTime Pro. This guide covers how to set up and manage your services.

---

## Creating a Service

1. Go to **Settings** → **Services** in your dashboard
2. Click **+ Add Service**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Service Name** — e.g., "AC Tune-Up", "Lawn Mowing", "Drain Cleaning"
   - **Description** — What the service includes (shown on quotes and booking page)
   - **Category** — Group related services (e.g., "Maintenance", "Repair", "Installation")
   - **Price** — Set a flat rate or hourly rate
   - **Duration** — Estimated time to complete
   - **Active** — Toggle whether it appears on your booking page

> **Tip:** Customers see your active services when booking online. Write clear descriptions to reduce back-and-forth questions.

---

## Service Categories

Organize your services into categories for easier navigation:

- **Maintenance** — Routine upkeep (tune-ups, inspections, cleaning)
- **Repair** — Fix and troubleshoot (leak repair, electrical fix, appliance repair)
- **Installation** — New equipment or features (AC install, fence build, fixture install)
- **Consultation** — On-site assessments and estimates
- **Emergency** — Urgent/after-hours services with premium pricing

Categories appear in your online booking page and help customers find the right service.

---

## Pricing Models

ToolTime Pro supports flexible pricing:

| Model | Best For | Example |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Flat Rate** | Standard jobs with predictable scope | "$150 — AC Tune-Up" |
| **Hourly Rate** | Variable jobs or time-and-materials | "$85/hour — General Plumbing" |
| **Custom Quote** | Complex jobs requiring site assessment | "Contact us for estimate" |

When creating quotes, you can override the default price for any service on a per-job basis.

## Smart Material Estimator

ToolTime Pro includes a **Smart Material Estimator** covering 21 trades:

- Painting, Plumbing, Electrical, Roofing, Fencing, Concrete, HVAC, Flooring, Drywall, Landscaping, and 11 more

**How it works:**
1. Describe the job (e.g., "Paint 3 bedrooms, 12x14 each")
2. Get an exact material list with **Good / Better / Best** pricing tiers
3. Apply your contractor markup automatically
4. Add the estimate directly to a quote

This helps you price jobs accurately and present professional options to customers.

---

## Online Booking Integration

Services marked as **Active** automatically appear on your public booking page:

- Customers visit `tooltimepro.com/book/[your-company-id]`
- They browse services by category, pick a time slot, and book
- The booking creates a lead and/or job in your dashboard
- Jenny AI can also book services via chat on your website

**Smart calendar** prevents double-bookings, and **automatic reminders** reduce no-shows.

---

## Bulk Service Management

For companies with many services:
- Use categories to keep things organized
- Toggle services on/off seasonally without deleting them
- Clone existing services to create variations (e.g., "Small Lawn" vs "Large Lawn")

---

## Best Practices

1. **Start with 5–10 core services** — You can always add more later
2. **Use clear, customer-facing names** — "AC Maintenance & Tune-Up" not "HVAC-MAINT-01"
3. **Include duration estimates** — Helps with scheduling and customer expectations
4. **Set competitive pricing** — Use the Material Estimator to validate your rates
5. **Review quarterly** — Update prices and descriptions as your business evolves
