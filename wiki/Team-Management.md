# Team Management

ToolTime Pro supports managing W-2 employees and 1099 contractors side by side with role-based permissions, GPS tracking, and compliance guardrails.

---

## Adding Team Members

1. Go to **Team** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Team Member**
3. Enter their name, email, phone, and role
4. Choose their worker classification:
   - **W-2 Employee** — Full employee with break tracking, overtime, and wage protections
   - **1099 Contractor** — Independent contractor with separate payment flows and classification guardrails
5. Click **Send Invite** — they'll receive an email to set up their account and download the worker app

---

## Roles & Permissions

| Role | Access Level |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything — billing, settings, reports, compliance, Jenny Exec |
| **Admin (Team Member + Admin)** | Manage jobs, team, customers, leads, quotes, invoices. No billing or account settings. |
| **Worker** | View assigned jobs, clock in/out, upload photos, report issues via the mobile app |

### Permission Controls
Owners can customize what each role can access:
- **Team management** — Add/edit workers
- **Customers** — View and manage customer records
- **Leads** — View and manage incoming leads
- **Quotes** — Create and send quotes
- **Invoicing** — Create and manage invoices
- **Time tracking** — View time logs and break records
- **Settings** — Access reports, webhooks, and account settings

## Blended Workforce Management

ToolTime Pro is built for businesses that use both employees and contractors:

### W-2 Employees
- Break tracking (meal and rest periods)
- California compliance alerts (AB5, wage laws)
- Overtime tracking
- GPS clock-in with location proof
- Photo verification (Elite plan)

### 1099 Contractors
- Separate payment tracking
- Classification guardrails to prevent misclassification
- Certification and license tracking
- Independent schedule management

### Multi-State Compliance
ToolTime Pro tracks labor laws across:
- **California** — AB5, meal/rest break rules, final wage requirements
- **Texas** — At-will employment, overtime rules
- **Florida** — Worker classification, wage laws
- **New York** — Freelance protection act, wage theft laws
- **Illinois** — Day labor rules, overtime regulations

See [ToolTime Shield](ToolTime-Shield) for full compliance features.

## Time Tracking

Workers clock in and out via the mobile app:
- **GPS clock-in** — Location proof at job start and end
- **Photo verification** (Elite) — Clock-in selfies for identity confirmation
- **Break tracking** — Meal and rest period logging with compliance alerts
- **Time logs** — Owners and admins view all time entries in the dashboard

---

## Best Practices

1. **Set roles carefully** — Give workers minimum necessary access
2. **Classify correctly** — Use ToolTime Shield's classification quiz if unsure about W-2 vs 1099
3. **Enable GPS clock-in** — Creates accountability and proof of work
4. **Review time logs weekly** — Catch overtime issues before they become costly
5. **Keep certifications current** — Track expiration dates for licenses and insurance
