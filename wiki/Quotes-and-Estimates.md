# Quotes & Estimates

ToolTime Pro's Smart Quoting system helps you create professional estimates, get customer approval with e-signatures, and convert quotes to jobs — all in minutes.

---

## Creating a Quote

1. Navigate to **Quotes** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ New Quote**
3. Select or create a customer
4. Add line items:
   - Choose from your **service catalog** or add custom items
   - Set quantity, unit price, and description for each line
   - Add materials from the **Smart Material Estimator** (21 trades supported)
5. Add optional notes, terms, or a personal message
6. Click **Send Quote** — the customer receives it via email

---

## Smart Quoting Features

### Three Ways to Create Quotes
- **Manual entry** — Select services, set prices, add notes
- **Voice input** — Describe the job and let Jenny AI draft the quote
- **Photo-based** — Upload a photo of the job site for AI-assisted estimation

### Material Estimator Integration
For trades like painting, plumbing, electrical, roofing, fencing, and 16 more:
1. Describe the scope (e.g., "Install 200 ft of 6-foot cedar fence")
2. Get a detailed material list with **Good / Better / Best** options
3. Apply your contractor markup
4. Add materials directly to the quote

### Professional Presentation
- Company logo and branding on every quote
- Itemized breakdown with descriptions
- Optional discount lines
- Tax calculation
- Expiration date
- Terms and conditions

---

## E-Signatures & Approval

When you send a quote, the customer receives a link to:

1. **Review** all line items and the total
2. **Approve** the quote with an electronic signature
3. **Request changes** if they need modifications

You'll receive a notification when a quote is approved or when changes are requested.

---

## Quote Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Quote created but not sent |
| **Sent** | Quote emailed to customer, awaiting response |
| **Viewed** | Customer opened the quote |
| **Approved** | Customer signed and approved |
| **Expired** | Past the expiration date without approval |
| **Declined** | Customer rejected the quote |

---

## Converting Quotes to Jobs

Once a quote is approved:

1. Open the approved quote
2. Click **Convert to Job**
3. Set a schedule date and assign a worker
4. The job is created with all quote details carried over
5. The customer receives a confirmation

This eliminates re-entering information and ensures pricing consistency.

---

## Quote Notifications

ToolTime Pro sends automatic notifications for:
- Quote sent to customer
- Customer viewed the quote
- Quote approved (with e-signature)
- Quote expired without action

All notifications appear in your dashboard bell icon and via email.

---

## Best Practices

1. **Respond quickly** — Send quotes within hours of an inquiry to close 3x faster
2. **Use the Material Estimator** — Accurate material costs build customer trust
3. **Set expiration dates** — Creates urgency (7–14 days is typical)
4. **Include clear scope** — Detail what's included and excluded to avoid disputes
5. **Follow up** — If a quote hasn't been viewed in 48 hours, send a reminder
