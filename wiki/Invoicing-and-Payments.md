# Invoicing & Payments

ToolTime Pro lets you create professional invoices, accept credit card payments, send automatic reminders, and sync everything with QuickBooks.

---

## Creating an Invoice

1. Go to **Invoices** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ New Invoice**
3. Select or create a customer
4. Add line items:
   - Pull from your **service catalog** for consistent pricing
   - Add custom line items for one-off charges
   - Include materials, labor, and any additional fees
5. Review totals (subtotal, tax, discount, total)
6. Click **Send Invoice** — the customer receives it via email with a payment link

### From a Quote
If you converted an approved quote to a job, you can generate an invoice directly from the completed job — all line items carry over automatically.

---

## Payment Methods

ToolTime Pro supports:

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Credit/Debit Cards** | Visa, Mastercard, Amex via Stripe — customers pay from their invoice link |
| **ACH Bank Transfer** | Lower fees for larger payments |
| **Manual Payment** | Record cash, check, or other offline payments |

Stripe handles all card processing securely. You'll need to connect your Stripe account in **Settings** → **Payments**.

---

## Invoice Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Created but not sent |
| **Sent** | Emailed to customer |
| **Viewed** | Customer opened the invoice |
| **Paid** | Payment received (full amount) |
| **Partial** | Partial payment received |
| **Overdue** | Past due date without full payment |

---

## Automatic Payment Reminders

ToolTime Pro sends reminders so you don't have to chase payments:

- **Before due date** — Gentle reminder 3 days before
- **On due date** — Payment due today notification
- **After due date** — Overdue reminders at 7, 14, and 30 days

Overdue invoices also appear in your dashboard stats and trigger a notification.

---

## Payment Plans

For larger jobs, offer customers installment payments:

1. Open an invoice
2. Click **Set Up Payment Plan**
3. Define the schedule (e.g., 3 monthly payments)
4. The customer sees a clear payment timeline
5. Automatic reminders go out before each installment

Payment Plans are available under the **Payment Plans** section in the sidebar.

## QuickBooks Sync

Keep your books in sync with two-way QuickBooks Online integration:

### What Syncs
- **Invoices** → QuickBooks invoices
- **Payments** → QuickBooks payment records
- **Customers** → QuickBooks customer list

### Setup
1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect QuickBooks**
3. Sign in to your QuickBooks Online account
4. Authorize the connection

### Pricing
- **Elite plan:** QuickBooks Sync included
- **Pro plan:** Available as add-on ($12/mo or $120/yr)
- **Starter plan:** Available as add-on ($12/mo or $120/yr)

## Invoice Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:
- Invoice paid (full or partial)
- Invoice overdue
- Customer viewed the invoice

Notifications appear in the dashboard bell and via email.

## Best Practices

1. **Invoice immediately after job completion** — Faster invoicing = faster payment
2. **Use the quote-to-invoice flow** — Eliminates re-entry errors
3. **Enable auto-reminders** — Reduces outstanding receivables significantly
4. **Connect QuickBooks** — Saves hours of manual bookkeeping
5. **Offer payment plans for big jobs** — Increases close rate on large projects
6. **Review overdue invoices weekly** — Follow up on aging receivables
