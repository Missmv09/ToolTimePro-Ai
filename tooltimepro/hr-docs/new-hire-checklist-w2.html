First Day ‚Äî Required Forms

‚ö†Ô∏è California Law: Complete These on Day 1

Failure to provide required notices can result in penalties. The Wage Theft Prevention Notice is required by Labor Code 2810.5.

  • Form I-9 (Section 1) Federal Day 1
    Employee completes Section 1 on first day. Employer completes Section 2 within 3 business days.
  • Form W-4 (Federal Tax Withholding) Federal
    Employee completes and signs. Keep on file for payroll.
  • Form DE-4 (CA State Tax Withholding) CA
    Required for California employees. Different from federal W-4.
  • Wage Theft Prevention Notice (DLSE Notice) CA Required At Hire
    MUST include: pay rate, pay day, employer info, overtime rates, sick leave info. Get signed copy.
  • Direct Deposit Authorization (optional)
    Employee may choose paper check instead. Cannot be mandatory in CA.
  • Emergency Contact Form
    Name, relationship, phone number for emergency situations.
